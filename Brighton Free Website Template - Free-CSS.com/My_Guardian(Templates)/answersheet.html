Q1. What is photosynthesis?
Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants use sunlight to make their own food using carbon dioxide and water.
Q2. Define the three states of matter.
The three states of matter are: Solid (fixed shape and volume), Liquid (fixed volume, variable shape), and Gas (no fixed shape or volume).
Q3. Write the formula for water and explain its composition.
The formula for water is H2O. It consists of two hydrogen atoms bonded to one oxygen atom.
